M11: DNSSEC Deployment in TLD and SLD

-

The M11 metrics analyze the Deployment of DNSSEC in TLDs and second level domains (such as "example.com") across 8 categories: all TLD, CC TLD, top 100, 101 to 1,000, 1,001 to 10,000, 10,001 to 100000, and 100,001 to 1M domains in the "Majectic Million" list, and names in the COM zone (See About M11 page for details.) For each category, we track the total deployment number and also the usage of specific DNS algorithms.
