EAI: Email Address Internationalization support in mail servers

-

The EAI metrics track the Email Address Internationalization (EAI) support on mail exchanger (MX) servers of second level domain names across participating Top Level Domains (TLDs). See About EAI for details. The data is presented in the EAI table below. The table includes one line per TLD, showing the MX records or registered in that TLD, or the IP addresses used by these MX records. The CSV files for previous measurements are available here. The table is quite large. The controls below can be used to navigate to specific slices of the data.
